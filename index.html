About Us

Knowledge Bridge is an internal learning and KT (Knowledge Transfer) platform designed to support freshers, lateral hires, and professionals transitioning into IT operations, monitoring, DevOps, and cloud roles.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between theory and real-world IT environments by providing structured KT documents, tool overviews, real-use scenarios, and operational best practices.

This platform helps:

  • ✅ New joiners understand tools faster
  • ✅ Reduce dependency on senior team members
  • ✅ Standardize KT documentation
  • ✅ Enable self-learning
